About us

Welcome to NextTechSquad, a dynamic scientific club at University Abdelhamid Mehri - Constantine 2, dedicated to exploring the exciting world of coding and cutting-edge technologies within the New Technologies of Information and Communication (NTIC) department. Our club brings together passionate students who are eager to delve into the latest advancements in fields like Artificial Intelligence, Web and Mobile Development, Cybersecurity, IoT, and Data Science. Through hands-on workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects, we aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications, empowering members with the skills and insights needed to thrive in the fast-evolving tech landscape. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, our community offers a supportive environment to learn, innovate, and connect with industry experts and like-minded peers. Join us as we push the boundaries of technology and shape the future, one line of code at a time.
